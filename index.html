About Mr.Art

Where Every Fragment
Tells a Story

Marco Ferretti
Master Mosaic Artist · Florence, Italy
🏆 12× Award Winner 🌍 18 Countries

I'm Marco — a passionate mosaic artist based in Florence, Italy. For over 14 years, I've been transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary expressions of color, texture, and meaning.

My work spans from intimate home interiors to sprawling garden installations, each piece hand-laid with Italian glass, natural stone, and ceramic tesserae. I believe mosaic is the oldest form of storytelling — and every home deserves its own story.

How It Works

From Vision to Mosaic

A seamless, collaborative journey from your first idea to the final installation. I guide you through every step with care and expertise.

01

Discovery Call

We discuss your space, vision, color palette, and budget. I listen deeply to understand the story you want your mosaic to tell.

02

Design Proposal

I create a detailed design mockup with material samples, color studies, and a full quote. You get to visualize the finished piece before a single tile is laid.

03

Handcrafted

The magic begins! Each tesserae is hand-cut and placed with precision. You receive photo updates throughout the creation process.

04

Installation & Care

Professional installation at your location, complete clean-up, and a full care guide. Plus 5-year craftsmanship guarantee on all work.
